Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get a response?

We try to respond to all feedback within 24-48 hours. Critical bug reports are prioritized.

Will you implement all feature requests?

We review all suggestions carefully and prioritize based on user demand and feasibility. Popular requests are more likely to be implemented.

Can I attach screenshots?

Yes! Screenshots are very helpful for bug reports. You can attach up to 5 files (10MB total).

Is my feedback private?

Your feedback is treated as confidential. We only use it to improve BlueChat and won't share it without your permission.
