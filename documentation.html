How to Use BlueChat

Follow this guide to get started with BlueChat and learn all its features.

Step 1: Installation

Installation

Download and install BlueChat from the Google Play Store or use the APK file directly.

Once installed, open the BlueChat app from your app drawer.

Step 2: Enable Bluetooth

Enable Bluetooth

Make sure Bluetooth is enabled on your device. BlueChat requires an active Bluetooth connection to work.

Go to Settings ‚Üí Bluetooth and turn it ON.

Step 3: Pair Devices

Pair Devices

Pair your device with another nearby device using Bluetooth. Open BlueChat on both devices and they will automatically discover each other.

Look for nearby devices in the BlueChat app and tap to connect.

Step 4: Create couple Chat

Create couple Chat

Connect with unique people to create a group. You can chat with up to 1 devices .

Tap the group icon and select devices to create a couple chat.

Step 5: Send & Receive Messages

Send & Receive Messages

Type your message in the message box and tap Send. Messages are instantly delivered to all connected devices.

Type and send messages in real-time. No internet needed!

Step 6: Share Files

Share Files (v2.1)

Share small files like images or documents through Bluetooth. Perfect for offline collaboration.

Tap the attachment icon to share files with your group.

Troubleshooting

Q: Why can't I see nearby devices?

A: Make sure Bluetooth is enabled on both devices and they are close to each other (within 10 meters). Also, ensure BlueChat has location permission enabled in Android settings.

Q: Messages are slow. What should I do?

A: Check your Bluetooth signal strength. Move closer to the other device or ensure there are no obstacles. Restart BlueChat if issues persist.

Q: Can I use BlueChat without internet?

A: Yes! BlueChat works completely offline using Bluetooth. Internet is only needed for downloading the app initially.

Q: How many people can I chat with?

A: BlueChat supports couple chats. Typically up to 1 devices can be connected simultaneously, depending on your device's Bluetooth capabilities.
